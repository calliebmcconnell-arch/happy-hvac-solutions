Happy HVAC Solutions LLC ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit happyhvacsolutions.com, use our services, or interact with us in other ways.

By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not access the site or use our services.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal InformationIncludes name, email address, phone number, billing address, and any other information you voluntarily provide when filling out forms or requesting services.
  • Non-Personal InformationIncludes data such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing activity. This data is collected through cookies and other tracking technologies.
  • Payment InformationWhen you make a purchase or payment for services, we may collect payment information such as credit card details. Payment information is processed by third-party payment processors and is not stored on our servers.
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Process and fulfill orders, and send confirmations.
  • Respond to customer service requests and support.
  • Improve our website and services through analytics.
  • Send promotional materials, updates, and marketing communications (you can opt out at any time).
Section 03

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers for the following purposes:

  • Payment processing.
  • Website hosting and analytics.
  • Email communication platforms.

These third parties have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Section 04

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and serve targeted advertisements. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

Section 05

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Section 06

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party websites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Section 07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing.
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
