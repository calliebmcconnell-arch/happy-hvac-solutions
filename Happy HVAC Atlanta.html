Common HVAC Issues

Atlanta HVAC problems & solutions

Recognize these symptoms early and you’ll save money. When in doubt, turn the system off and call us — running a struggling unit usually makes the repair worse.

Issue 01

AC blowing warm air

The Problem

Your AC is running but blowing warm air, especially on hot Atlanta days when you need it most.

Common Causes

Low refrigerant (leak), dirty condenser coil, clogged air filter, compressor failure.

What to Do

Turn off the AC and call us at 770-364-5141. Don’t run the system — it can cause more damage. We’ll diagnose and repair same-day if possible.

Issue 02

AC won’t start

The Problem

Your air conditioner won’t turn on, and temperatures in your Atlanta home are rising.

Common Causes

Thermostat malfunction, tripped breaker, blown fuse, capacitor failure.

What to Do

Check thermostat settings. Verify your breaker hasn’t tripped. If it still won’t start, call us for emergency service.

Issue 03

Furnace blowing cold air

The Problem

Your furnace is running but blowing cold air instead of heat during Georgia’s cold months.

Common Causes

Thermostat set to cool, limit switch failure, burner malfunction, ignition failure.

What to Do

Check your thermostat is set to heat mode. If it still blows cold, turn off the furnace and call us — don’t keep running it.

Issue 04

Strange noises

The Problem

Your HVAC system is making unusual grinding, squealing, or banging noises that concern you.

Common Causes

Loose parts, belt wear, bearing failure, compressor problems, debris in system.

What to Do

Turn off the system immediately and call us. Strange noises indicate mechanical problems that need professional repair before they get worse.

Issue 05

System short-cycling

The Problem

Your AC or furnace keeps turning on and off frequently, wasting energy.

Common Causes

Oversized equipment, dirty filter, thermostat malfunction, airflow restriction.

What to Do

Check and replace your air filter first. If short-cycling continues, call us — this wastes energy and wears out equipment faster.

Issue 06

High energy bills

The Problem

Your heating and cooling bills seem too high for your Atlanta home’s size.

Common Causes

Aging or inefficient equipment, poor insulation, system running constantly, refrigerant leak, dirty filters.

What to Do

Schedule maintenance to check system efficiency. If your HVAC is over 15 years old, replacement may save money vs. continued high bills.
