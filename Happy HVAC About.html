Our Story

A family business with deep Marietta roots

Happy HVAC Solutions LLC is a full-service HVAC company based in Marietta, Georgia servicing the Atlanta & North Georgia area. We are family-owned and operated with deep roots based in Marietta, GA. We love supporting our community and serving our customers daily.

We specialize in residential & commercial HVAC installation, maintenance, repair, design & enhancement services. Our team of certified technicians is committed to providing exceptional service — and we operate our business under the principles of honesty, integrity, and dedication to our customers.

Happy HVAC Solutions prides itself on being a "one stop shop" that can diagnose, repair, install, maintain & upgrade significant pieces of equipment, keeping homes & businesses running with minimal delay. For commercial HVAC-R, we focus on commercial, light industrial, & restaurant refrigeration — supporting all aspects of your home or business HVAC-R & plumbing systems, ensuring they're happy.
