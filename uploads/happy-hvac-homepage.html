Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve? ▼
We serve the greater Atlanta metro area and North Georgia, including Marietta, Alpharetta, Roswell, Kennesaw, Woodstock, Acworth, Smyrna, Canton, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, Johns Creek, and Cumming. Not sure if you're in our area? Call us at 770-364-5141.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency service? ▼
Yes. We offer 24/7 emergency HVAC service including nights, weekends, and holidays. If your AC goes out in the middle of a Georgia summer or your heat stops working in winter, call us at 770-364-5141 and we'll get someone out as quickly as possible.
What brands do you service? ▼
We service, repair, and install all major HVAC brands. You don't need to purchase equipment through us — we're happy to install units that customers purchase directly. Just give us a call and we'll let you know if we can service your specific system.
What is the Happy Home Comfort Club? ▼
Our Comfort Club is an affordable annual maintenance membership starting at $20.50/month. Members receive priority scheduling, annual tune-ups, discounts on repairs, and a 100% satisfaction guarantee. It's the easiest way to prevent breakdowns and extend the life of your HVAC system.
Do you offer financing options? ▼
Yes, we offer financing options to help make HVAC replacement and installation projects more manageable. Visit our financing page or call us to discuss what options may be available for your project.
How quickly can you respond to a service call? ▼
For emergency calls, we aim to respond as quickly as possible — often same day. For standard service requests submitted through our website, we typically follow up within one hour during business hours to schedule your appointment at a time that works for you.
