FAQ

Commonly asked questions

Why is HVAC maintenance important?+
HVAC maintenance keeps your heating and cooling systems functioning efficiently, prolongs their lifespan, and saves money on utility costs. Just like your car, your AC, heating, plumbing, and electrical systems need routine tune-ups to keep operating at peak performance. Without regular servicing, these systems waste energy and break down more frequently.
How often should HVAC systems be maintained?+
HVAC systems should be maintained at least twice a year — ideally in the spring (before cooling season) and fall (before heating season) — to ensure optimal performance year-round.
What does HVAC maintenance involve?+
HVAC maintenance typically involves cleaning or replacing filters, checking and tightening electrical connections, lubricating moving parts, inspecting and cleaning coils, and testing system controls.
Can HVAC maintenance prevent breakdowns?+
Regular HVAC maintenance helps prevent breakdowns by identifying and addressing potential issues before they escalate into major problems — keeping your system reliable when you need it most.
What are the benefits of joining a maintenance plan?+
Joining the Happy Home Comfort Club provides priority service, discounted repair pricing (15% off), no after-hours charges, no service-call fees, twice-yearly tune-ups, and member-only pricing on additional services.
Is HVAC maintenance covered by warranty?+
HVAC maintenance is typically not covered by manufacturer warranty, as it’s considered routine upkeep rather than a repair or replacement service. However, most equipment warranties require proof of regular maintenance to remain valid — another reason a Comfort Club membership is a smart investment.
